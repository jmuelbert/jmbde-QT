2. Getting Started¶

2.1. Opening jmbde¶

|logo| Once you’ve installed jmbde, start by opening jmbde add data…

Windows

Double-click the jmbde icon on the Desktop. Alternatively, browse your Windows start menu and click the jmbde icon, or perform a search for jmbde.exe.

Mac OSX

Double-click the jmbde icon in the Applications folder. Alternatively, drag the jmbde icon to the dock and double-click it there or search for jmbde in Spotlight.

GNU/Linux

Click the jmbde icon in the applications menu or launcher of your desktop environment or perform a search for jmbde. Alternatively type jmbde into the terminal, then hit Return.
