PLACEHOLDER¶

SYNOPSIS¶

jmbde [-h]

DESCRIPTION¶

jmbde is a program for the management of resources in companies. with this program, employees and the equipment they need for their work can be recorded. These are computers, printers and telephones. Complete configuration information is available online at https://jmuelbert.github.io/jmbde-QT/.
